How It Works

It's All About The Health Score:

Every searchable food and beverage in our database, aka the USDA Food Composition Databases, once searched, is assigned a Health Score. The maximum Health Score for any food is 100 points. Every food starts off with 75 Health Score points before encountering our algorithm, humbly named, the Optimal Nutrition Assurance Algorithm, or ONAA for short. Ultimately, it is ONAA's job to give each food and/or beverage a final Health Score which is then used to determine the final answer. A Health Score of 100 points indicates the healthiest possible food you could eat while a Health Score of 1 point represents the worst possible food you could eat. Such is the importance of the soon-to-be legendary Health Score.
